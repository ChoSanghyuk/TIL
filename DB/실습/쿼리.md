## 테이블 컬럼 조회



### PK 컬럼 여부 조회

```sql
SELECT A.TABLE_NAME, A.COLUMN_NAME, NVL(C.CONSTRAINT_TYPE, 'N') AS CONSTRAINT_TYPE, A.DATA_TYPE
  FROM ALL_TAB_COLUMNS A 
LEFT JOIN ALL_CONS_COLUMNS B ON A.OWNER = B.OWNER AND A.TABLE_SCHEMA = B.CONSTRAINT_SCHEMA 
	AND A.TABLE_NAME = B.TABLE_NAME AND A.COLUMN_NAME = B.COLUMN_NAME
LEFT JOIN ALL_CONSTRAINTS C ON A.TABLE_NAME = C.TABLE_NAME AND A.TABLE_SCHEMA = C.CONSTRAINT_SCHEMA 
	AND B.CONSTRAINT_NAME = C.CONSTRAINT_NAME
WHERE 1=1
  AND A.OWNER = 계정
  AND A.TABLE_NAME = 테이블명
```
